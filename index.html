Mark Williamson

I'm a Predoctoral Fellow with the Evidence in Governance and Politics network at Toronto Metropolitan University and a PhD candidate (ABD) in the Department of Politics at New York University.

I write on Canadian and comparative politics, with a focus on how members of dominant groups think about historical injustices committed against out-groups.

Before my PhD, I completed an M.A. in Political Science at McGill University and a Bachelor's degree at the University of Waterloo.
