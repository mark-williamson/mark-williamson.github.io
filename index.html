Mark Williamson

I'm a PhD student in the Department of Politics at New York University. My research focuses on political violence, dictatorship and democratization. My current projects investigate the dynamics of state repression, nonviolent protest and regime change, with a regional emphasis on Latin America.

Before coming to NYU, I completed an M.A. in Political Science at McGill University and a Bachelor's degree at the University of Waterloo.
